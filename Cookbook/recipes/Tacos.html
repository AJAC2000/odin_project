Tacos!

Description

Tacos, a staple of Mexican cuisine, offer a wide array of flavors and textures. They typically consist of a corn or wheat tortilla filled with various savory ingredients like grilled meats (such as carne asada or al pastor), fresh vegetables, salsas, guacamole, and cheese, allowing for endless combinations and regional variations.

Ingredients

  • Corn or flour tortillas
  • Grilled meats (e.g., carne asada, al pastor) or other protein options
  • Fresh vegetables (e.g., lettuce, tomatoes, onions)
  • Salsa (e.g., pico de gallo, salsa verde, salsa roja)
  • Guacamole
  • Cheese (e.g., queso fresco, cheddar)
  • Optional: Cilantro, lime wedges

Steps

  1. Warm the tortillas by heating them in a dry skillet or on a grill until soft and pliable.
  2. Grill or cook the meats until they are fully cooked and tender.
  3. Prepare the fresh vegetables by washing and chopping them into desired sizes.
  4. Heat the tortillas and assemble by placing the grilled meat or protein of choice in the center.
  5. Top with fresh vegetables, salsa, guacamole, and cheese.
  6. Add a squeeze of lime juice and sprinkle with cilantro if desired.
  7. Fold the tortillas over the filling, creating a taco shape.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  9. Serve the tacos warm and enjoy!
