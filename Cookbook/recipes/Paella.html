Paella!

Description

Paella is a vibrant and aromatic rice dish that hails from Spain, specifically the Valencia region. This one-pot wonder showcases a symphony of flavors, combining saffron-infused rice with an assortment of ingredients such as chicken, rabbit, seafood, vegetables, and spices, all slowly cooked together to create a harmonious medley.

Ingredients

  • Arborio or short-grain rice
  • Chicken pieces (e.g., thighs, drumsticks)
  • Rabbit meat (optional)
  • Seafood (e.g., shrimp, mussels, clams)
  • Onion, chopped
  • Bell peppers (red and green), sliced
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Saffron threads
  • Paprika
  • Chicken broth
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large paella pan or wide skillet over medium heat.
  2. Sear the chicken pieces and rabbit meat (if using) until browned on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add more oil if needed and saut√© the onions, bell peppers, and garlic until softened.
  4. Add the rice to the pan and stir to coat the grains with oil.
  5. Sprinkle saffron threads and paprika over the rice, and stir well to distribute the flavors.
  6. Return the chicken and rabbit meat to the pan and pour in the chicken broth. Season with salt to taste.
  7. Simmer over medium-low heat without stirring for about 10 minutes.
  8. Arrange the seafood on top of the rice, pushing them slightly into the mixture.
  9. Continue to cook for another 10-15 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the liquid has absorbed, making sure the bottom doesn't burn.
  10. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  11. Garnish with lemon wedges and serve directly from the pan.
