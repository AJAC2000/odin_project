Pizza!

Description

Originating from Naples, Italy, pizza has become a beloved international dish. The traditional Neapolitan pizza features a thin, chewy crust topped with simple yet flavorful ingredients such as fresh mozzarella cheese, San Marzano tomatoes, basil leaves, and extra-virgin olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed San Marzano tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Optional: Toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible (usually around 500°F or 260°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape.
  3. Place the rolled-out dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce or crushed San Marzano tomatoes evenly over the dough.
  5. Tear or slice fresh mozzarella cheese and distribute it evenly over the sauce.
  6. Add any additional desired toppings.
  7. Drizzle a little extra-virgin olive oil over the pizza.
  8. Sprinkle salt and torn basil leaves on top.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, and slice before serving.
